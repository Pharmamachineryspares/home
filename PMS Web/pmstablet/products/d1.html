EU & TSM Specifications

Pharma Machinery & Spares accommodates both standards of B, D, BB, BBS tooling configurations used in today’s International Market. The American Standard known as the TSM Standard and the European Standard known as the EU or Euro norm Standard. TSM and EU tooling standards were established in the industry to provide a structure that protects companies tooling investments while maintaining established processes and procedures. TSM and EU configurations are very similar to each other but cannot be interchanged.
