Double Rotary High Speed Tablet Press/Compression Machine with Pre-compression

Features of High Speed Tablet Press /Compression Machine with Pre-compression:


It is Double Rotary Tablet Press suitable for large batch production having capacity of higher table output with full consideration to GMP standards and available toolings are D B BB Punch Die Sets.

  • High speed rotary Tablet Press/Compression Machine with Pre-compression suitable for large batch production having capacity of higher tablet output with full consideration to GMP standards.
  • Square & elegant design with cabinet top of stainless steel 304. The design is operator friendly and comfortable to the operator while the production.
  • Facility for fine tuning all parameters during operation of the press with the help of outside front controls with dials.
  • For the ease of operation the press is equipped with round hoppers of stainless steel with butterfly valve.
  • The press has Pre-compression facility, ACVF drive for the main motor as well as force feeding motor, which enable operator to synchronize press feed with feeding.
  • The press has electromagnetic clutch arrangements with helical gear ring to drive the turret.
  • Specially designed pressure link assembly, which ensures sufficient & equal pressure to the tablets for maintaining tablet weight consistency.
  • The press has auto lubrication system.
  • Standard press has one piece turret of special grade casting. However 3 piece turret with SS- 304/316 middle plate is also available (optionally). Electro less nickel plated turret can be also provided (optionally).
  • Lower punch seals are provided at lower punch hole in turret.
  • The press has ball bearing type upper & lower pressure rolls. Upper rolls carries are MS fabricated with hard chrome plating.
  • Upper guards for the tableting section are made of poly carbonate with stainless hinges & handles, sturdy with aluminum frame & fitted with gas filled shock absorbers. The lower guards of the press are with excellent matt finish. All guards are interlocked for safety.
  • There is not any painted parts in tablet manufacturing zone as all parts are made of stainless material or gunmetal or of hard chrome plated.
  • Detachable control panel is provided for all electrical controls on the top. Acrylic cover is also provided to protect controls from dust. The press has anti vibrating mounts and excellent paint other than tablet manufacturing zone wherever it is required.
  • Excellent bottom driven force feeding system for material feeding. Gravity type feeding can be provided optionally. Two layer / bilayer attachment can also be provided optionally.
  • Designed with a view to minimize change over time. The weight adjustment section is easily removable which provides easy access to lower punch die sets for the removal / replacement of lower punch die sets.
  • The hand wheel is easily located for indexing the turret during tool change over by a single operator.
  • Dust extractor nozzle is specially designed which can be connected with a dust extractor keeping it away from press.
  • Hydraulic power pack for pressure loading and overload release. A hydraulic gauge is also provided on lower guard for easy view at an additional cost.
