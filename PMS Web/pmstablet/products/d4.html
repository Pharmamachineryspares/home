Dust Cups & Bellows

We recommend using dust cups with the punches to provide a secondary barrier to the upper punch seals. This also prevents oil & other debris from contaminating granulation and ensures production runs smoothly and cut back on cleaning time.

All the dust cups are made with approved pharmaceutical-grade material that are specifically selected for their stability, wear-resistance, cleaning & flexibility. While these are an optional accessory, their benefits far outweigh their price.
