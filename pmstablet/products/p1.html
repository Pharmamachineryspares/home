Single Rotary Mini Tablet Press / Compression Machine

The Single Rotary Mini Tablet Press / Compression Machine, is an ideal choice for R&D work as well as small scale productions. It is also used to avoid wastage when high value ingredients are used for tablet formulations, also known as lab tablet press/compression Machine.

The Single Rotary Mini Tablet Press / Compression Machine is designed with GMP considerations. It is sturdy, fitted with single sided turret of special grade Iron Casting

Features of Single Rotary Mini Tablet Press / Compression Machine:

  • Pre compression facility as a standard feature
  • A. C. variable drive and fixed pulley. It is driven from central pillar by worm reduction gear box.
  • Lower guards of stainless steel & upper guards of transparent acrylic to view tablet compression process of the machine comfortably.
  • Electronic Tablet Counting System
  • Granules are fed through an open feed frame which ensures trouble free settings and minimum wastage of valuable material.
  • Force feeding attachment is optional accessories for difficult, fluffy & fine granule compression.
  • Table top, Mobile & easy to cleanTable top, Mobile & easy to clean
  • For easy operation outside controls are given for Pre Compression adjustment, tablet thickness adjustment and turret speed adjustment

Optional Parts/ Accessories available with the Single Rotary Mini Tablet Press /Compression Machine

  • Available in 4D+4B / 4D+4B+4BB model
  • SS 316 Contact Parts
  • Nickel Plating on Turret
  • Hopper Granule Level Sensor
  • Force Feeder Attachment
  • Stand with Drawer (SS 304)
